Strawberry Cheesecake Ice Cream

Esimpraim. Strawberry Cheesecake Ice Cream. June 9th, 2010. Flickr.com. https://www.flickr.com/photos/dishingupdelights/4687493452/

Description

Strawberry cheesecake ice cream is a flavour of ice cream that tastes like strawberry cheesecake. The ingredients it contains depend on the recipe, but generally includes strawberries, sugar, and cream cheese. This is the perfect treat for cheesecake lovers!
