Description

Apple pie is a baked dessert that consists of a wheat crust with a soft‚ sweet apple‑and‑sugar filling. It is usually served warm.

History

Although apple pie is considered an American icon‚ its origins actually lie in Europe. The first recorded recipe came from England during the late 1300s and also included figs‚ saffron (a spice)‚ raisins‚ and pears. In addition to that‚ old versions of the dessert lacked sugar. Crusts were not eaten; they were simply meant to hold the filling. Now‚ the whole pie is meant for eating ‐ crust and all!

Where to Taste

Apple pie can be found prepackaged at many North American grocery stores‚ such as Walmart and FreshCo. These pies generally need only be baked as per the instructions on the box. You can also find recipes via baking websites and library cookbooks.
