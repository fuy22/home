Japan

Japan is a country located in Eastern Asia. Its capital city is Tokyo and the official language is Japanese. As of 2021, Japan's population consists of about 125 million people.

Popular Japanese Dishes:

  • Sushi
  • Onigiri (rice balls)
  • Miso soup
  • Various types of noodles, including udon and ramen
  • Okonomiyaki (a type of pancake with ingredients, such as meat, combined with it
